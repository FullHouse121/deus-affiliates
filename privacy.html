Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

1. What this covers

This policy describes how DEUS Affiliates (part of DEUSMACHINE) handles personal data collected through this website and the partner program.

2. Data we collect

  • Application data — name, Telegram or email contact, traffic sources and GEOs you submit through the partner form;
  • Partnership data — payment details you provide for payouts, statistics generated by your tracking links;
  • Technical data — language preference stored locally in your browser (localStorage), and standard server logs.

3. How we use it

To review applications, operate the partnership (offers, tracking, payouts), communicate with you about your account, and comply with legal obligations including anti-fraud and anti-money-laundering checks.

4. Cookies and local storage

The website stores your language choice in your browser's local storage. It does not set advertising cookies of its own. If analytics tools are enabled, they may set their own identifiers; you can block these with standard browser controls.

5. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share it only with service providers needed to run the program (hosting, form processing, payment providers) and where required by law.

6. Retention

Application data is kept while your application or partnership is active and for as long afterwards as law requires. You can ask us to delete your application data at any time.

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. Write to us and we will respond within a reasonable time.

8. Contact

Privacy questions and requests: partners@deusaffiliates.com.
